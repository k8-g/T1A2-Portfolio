Hi!


I'm Kate & I'm a purple-haired, deaf goth learning to code.
Based in Brisbane, I'm looking to join the tech industry as I've been told it's a disability-friendly industry.
Having grown up using computers since before I could read (with the help of my older brothers reading out what's on the screen to me), I've dabbled in technology and coding a little over the years, building my own computer once my Dad taught me at age 10 and playing with basic HTML & CSS codes over the years. It's been a long time since I learnt anything new in coding, so here I am, undertaking this diploma in the hopes to gain employment in the tech industry next year.
